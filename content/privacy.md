## 1. Information We Collect

We collect information in three main ways: information you provide, information collected automatically, and information from third-party sign-in services.

### 1.1 Information You Provide

When you create an account, subscribe, or contact us, we may collect:

- **Email address** — required for account creation, magic-link sign-in, and transactional messages
- **Display name** — if you choose to set one or if provided through Google sign-in
- **Profile photo** — if you sign in with Google OAuth and choose to share your Google profile image
- **Payment-related information** — when you subscribe, payment details (such as card number, billing address, and payment method type) are collected and processed directly by **Stripe**. We do not store full card numbers on our servers. We may receive from Stripe your customer ID, subscription status, billing period dates, and limited billing metadata needed to manage your account
- **Communications** — if you email us for support, account requests, or privacy inquiries

If you complete checkout as a guest without an existing account, we create an account using the email address you provide in Stripe Checkout.

### 1.2 Information Collected Automatically

When you use the Service, we and our service providers may automatically collect:

- **Watch history and progress** — which episodes you watch, how far you watched, and when you last watched (to resume playback and improve recommendations)
- **Watchlist data** — series you save to your list
- **Device and browser information** — browser type, operating system, device type, and general technical identifiers needed to deliver video and keep the Service secure
- **IP address** — used for security, fraud prevention, approximate location (country/region level), and service diagnostics
- **Usage data** — pages viewed, features used, referral URLs, and interaction with the player (such as play, pause, and completion events)
- **Log data** — server logs from our hosting provider that may include timestamps, request paths, and error reports

### 1.3 Information from Google OAuth

If you choose “Continue with Google,” Google may share with us:

- Your Google account email address
- Your name (if available in your Google profile)
- Your profile picture URL (if available)
- A unique identifier associated with your Google account

We use this information only to authenticate you and personalize your account. Google’s use of your data is governed by [Google’s Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy).

### 1.4 Cookies and Similar Technologies

We use cookies and similar storage technologies primarily for **authentication and session management** through Supabase. These help keep you signed in and protect your account.

We do **not** currently use advertising cookies or sell your personal information for cross-context behavioral advertising. We may use essential cookies and local storage required for the Service to function.

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling essential cookies may prevent you from signing in or using subscribed features.
